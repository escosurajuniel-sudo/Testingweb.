About This Website

This website is designed as a simple yet modern 5-page template. It demonstrates how a clean structure and professional design can be applied to personal, business, or portfolio websites.

The site includes a Home page with a welcoming message, an About page explaining its purpose, a Services page outlining offerings, a Portfolio page to display projects, and a Contact page for easy communication.

Its main goal is to provide a solid foundation for anyone who wants a responsive, professional-looking website without complexity. The design ensures readability, usability, and a modern aesthetic that works seamlessly across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
